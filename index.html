Leef & Maggie's Minecraft 2D

WASD / Arrows to move & jump | 1-9 select slot | E crafting

Left Click mine/attack | Right Click place block / use flint & steel | F interact (eat / sleep / trade / open chest / open door)

R respawn | M toggle music | Click items in inventory to move them around!

Craft Torches (1 Stick + 1 Coal = 4) to light up the night and stop mob spawns!

Craft a Bed (3 Planks + 3 Leaves) to sleep through the night!

Explore caves underground for ores! Mine copper, iron, gold, and diamonds!

Build a 4x5 obsidian frame and use Flint & Steel to open a Nether portal!

Craft guns! Pistol (2 Iron + 1 Copper + 1 Gunpowder) or AK-47 (4 Iron + 2 Copper + 1 Gunpowder)

Watch out for Husks in the desert and Endermen who teleport!
